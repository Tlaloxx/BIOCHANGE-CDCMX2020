SARS-CoV-2

What is SARS-CoV-2?

Coronaviruses (CoVs) are a large family of viruses that cause illnesses ranging from the common cold to more serious illnesses. This virus is of great relevance because it spread throughout the world, which is why it was declared by the OMS as a public health emergency of international importance on January 30, 2020. This pandemic is wreaking havoc on its way, that is why we are committed with the creation of scientific and technological solutions to stop it.

SOLUTION

The solution is intended for rapid implementation. As output from the system we have an electrical signal that we can calibrate to determine the percentage of virus acceptable to generate a positive diagnosis. The amount of virus contained in the sample is proportional to the electrical output signal.
