What about this?

This system consists in a lab-on-a-chip where our sample is a nasopharyngeal swab in saline solution. This sample is deposited in the sample spot (1) and let the sample go over all the microfluidic length (2). Finally the sample ends in a spot where we previously set our molecule quantum dot-antibody(QDs-Ab) solution and the two solutions mix (3), QDs-Ab binded with the SARS-CoV-2 spike protein will be excited with an UV led and the photoreceptor will capture the fluorescence and analyze the sample in order to deliver a COVID positive or negative result [figure 1].
